About Me

I'm a UX Designer and researcher pursuing a dual degree in UX Design and Political Science at Purdue University, with minors in Russian, Anthropology, and Management. My work bridges cultural understanding with technical execution, creating digital experiences that resonate across boundaries.

My interdisciplinary background allows me to approach design challenges with a unique lens—combining user research methodologies, linguistic insights, and cultural awareness to create solutions that are both aesthetically compelling and functionally robust.

Currently, I'm designing digital portfolios, researching literary influences across cultures, and prototyping solutions that enhance user experience in academic and commercial settings.
